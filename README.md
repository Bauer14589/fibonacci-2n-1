## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the Fibonacci sequence?

The Fibonacci sequence is a series of numbers where each number is the sum of the two preceding ones. It begins with 0 and 1, leading to 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, and so forth.

### How does the Binet formula work?

The Binet formula offers a way to calculate Fibonacci numbers without relying on recursion. It uses golden ratio properties to quickly determine the value.

### Can I use the app offline?

Yes, once installed on your system, the application works without needing an internet connection.
